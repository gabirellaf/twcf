Drives & Events

2026 Drive Calendar

Active — Summer 2026

The Comfort & Care Drive

Collecting hygiene products, food, educational materials, and household essentials for women, children, and families experiencing hardship, health-related needs, housing instability, DV recovery, or postpartum need.

  • Confirm venue for kit assembly
  • Confirm at least 3 drop-off locations (churches, businesses)
  • Design and print flyers (Canva)
  • Post on Instagram + Facebook
  • Confirm recipient organization(s)
  • Schedule volunteer assembly day
  • Arrange pick-up vehicle/driver
Upcoming — Fall 2026

Back-to-School Drive

Collecting school supplies and educational materials for students in need. Target: Lansing School District Title I schools, Head Start programs.

  • Contact Lansing School District re: partnership
  • Contact Head Start of Ingham County
  • Set dates (target: August 2026)
  • Confirm supply list and collection plan
Upcoming — Winter 2026

Hospital Toy Drive

Collecting toys and comfort items for children in pediatric wings of local hospitals. Call to ask for Child Life Specialist or volunteer coordinator.

  • Call Sparrow Hospital pediatric unit — (517) 364-1000
  • Call McLaren Greater Lansing pediatrics — (517) 975-6000
  • Confirm what items are most needed
  • Set collection dates and drop-off plan
Planning — Summer 2026

Summer Toy Drive

Summer toys, water toys, bathing suits, sunscreen, bug spray, books, backpacks. Starting ~2–3 weeks from now.

  • Contact Boys & Girls Club of Lansing
  • Contact YMCA of Metropolitan Lansing
  • Contact Head Start of Ingham County
  • Contact Child and Family Services of Michigan (Lansing)
  • Post call for donations on social media
  • Set collection window and distribution plan
Supply Reference

Full Accepted Donations List

Always Accepted

  • Hygiene products (shampoo, soap, deodorant, toothpaste, feminine products)
  • Medical supplies (gauze, bandages, Neosporin, pain relievers, thermometers)
  • Non-perishable food (canned goods, pasta, rice, peanut butter, cereal)
  • Children's books
  • School supplies (pencils, notebooks, crayons, folders, backpacks)
  • New socks and underwear (all sizes)

Drive-Specific

  • Toys (new or gently used — no broken pieces)
  • Clothing (clean, gently used — note size on bag)
  • Summer: bathing suits, sunscreen, bug spray, water toys, towels
  • Hospital: new toys only, no stuffed animals with loose parts
  • Back-to-school: calculators, folders, educational workbooks
  • Baby items: formula, diapers, wipes, onesies
