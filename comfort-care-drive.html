About the Drive

Who We're Serving.

The Comfort & Care Drive supports women, children, and families in the Lansing and Okemos area who are experiencing:

  • ✓Domestic violence recovery
  • ✓Housing instability or homelessness
  • ✓Pregnancy or postpartum need
  • ✓Health-related hardship or medical need
  • ✓Food insecurity
  • ✓Basic household need

Kits are distributed through vetted partner organizations — shelters, domestic violence centers, and family support services — ensuring every donation reaches someone who needs it.

How to Donate

Three Easy Steps.

1

Choose your kit edition

Pick the kit that speaks to you — or just donate whatever individual items you have. Every contribution helps.

2

Gather or purchase the items

Use the checklist above as your guide. All items should be new. If possible, place completed kits in a clear bag or small box with the kit edition name written on it.

3

Drop off or mail your donation

Mail to PO Box 832, East Lansing, MI 48826 — or contact us to find out where to drop off in person. New drop-off locations are being confirmed and will be announced soon.
