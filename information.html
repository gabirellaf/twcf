The Issues We Address

Why this work matters.

Millions of women and children face domestic violence, food insecurity, lack of healthcare access, and housing instability every year. Many don't know where to turn — or are too afraid to ask for help.

The Women and Children Foundation exists to bridge that gap — providing immediate relief through care kits and connecting people to verified, trustworthy resources for every type of need.

Domestic Violence

1 in 4 women experience severe intimate partner violence. Safe shelter and support can save lives.

Food Insecurity

Women-headed households are disproportionately affected by food insecurity. Access to nutrition is a basic right.

Education Access

Children in crisis often miss out on the school supplies and stability they need to learn and grow.

Healthcare Access

Many women and children in crisis lack access to basic medical care and health information.
