Care Kit Editions

Choose a kit edition based on who you want to support. Every item goes directly to a woman or child in need — through our partner organizations.

Pick your edition.

Each edition is designed for a specific need. You can make one or several — any kit you assemble makes a real difference.

Edition 6

First Aid & Medical Kit

Basic medical supplies for women and families without access to regular healthcare.

Best for: shelters, healthcare outreach, families in medical need
ItemQuantity
Bandages (assorted)1 box
Gauze pads4–6
Medical tape1 roll
Antiseptic wipes1 pack
Neosporin or antibiotic ointment1
