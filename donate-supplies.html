Can't make it to a drive? You can mail donations directly to us. Fill out the form below so we know what's coming — then ship your items to our address.

What We Accept

Items We're Looking For.

Hygiene

Shampoo, conditioner, soap, deodorant, toothpaste, toothbrushes, feminine hygiene products, lotion

Medical Supplies

Gauze, bandages, Neosporin, pain relievers, thermometers, first aid basics

Non-Perishable Food

Canned goods, pasta, rice, peanut butter, cereal, snacks, baby food

Toys & Books

New or gently used toys, children's books, games, puzzles, stuffed animals

School & Educational Supplies

Backpacks, notebooks, pencils, crayons, markers, folders, calculators, educational workbooks

Clothing

New or gently used clothing for women and children of all sizes — especially coats, socks, and underwear (new only)
