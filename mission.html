Mission

Why We Exist.

The Women and Children Foundation exists to support women and children in crisis — connecting them to trusted help and creating pathways toward safety, health, education, and long-term stability.

We start locally, making impact visible and real. Every care kit, every referral, every partnership is a step toward a world where no woman or child falls through the cracks.

Vision

Where We're Headed.

A future where every woman and child — regardless of circumstances — has access to the care, safety, and opportunity they deserve.

  • ✓ Scholarships and educational support
  • ✓ PSAs on domestic violence, harassment, and assault safety
  • ✓ Health resources led by women-centered medical voices
  • ✓ A verified network of safe homes for survivors
  • ✓ Global awareness and relief pathways
Our Values

What Guides Everything We Do.

Dignity

Every person we serve deserves to be treated with respect, compassion, and care — without judgment.

Safety

We prioritize the physical and emotional safety of every woman and child we work with, always.

Community

Real change happens through trusted relationships — with shelters, partners, volunteers, and the people we serve.
