How It Works

From you to someone in need.

Every action — big or small — moves directly through our foundation to reach women and children.

1

Donate or Join

Donate supplies, become a member, or sign up to volunteer — every contribution fuels the work.

2

We Assemble

Volunteers and staff pack care kits with hygiene products, food, and school supplies — ready to go to people who need them.

3

It Reaches Them

Care kits are distributed through partner organizations — shelters, hospitals, schools, and community centers — to women and children in need.

Current Initiative · Summer 2026

The Comfort & Care Drive

Supporting women, children, and families experiencing hardship, health-related needs, housing instability, domestic violence recovery, pregnancy or postpartum need, or basic household need — through hygiene products, food, and educational materials.
