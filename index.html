What We Do

Relief, Connection, and Hope.

Real, visible impact — locally rooted and built to reach every woman and child who needs us.

Care Kits

Hygiene products, food, school supplies, and essentials — assembled and distributed through trusted partner organizations.

Community Partnerships

We work with shelters, domestic violence organizations, and healthcare providers to reach people safely and responsibly.

Education & Outreach

Building awareness around domestic violence, sexual assault, and women's health — and connecting people with verified resources.

Current Initiative · Summer 2026

The Comfort & Care Drive

Supporting women, children, and families experiencing hardship, health-related needs, housing instability, domestic violence recovery, pregnancy or postpartum need, or basic household need — through hygiene products, food, and educational materials.
